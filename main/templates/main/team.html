Meet the Team!

The OpenCovid survey was created by a team of scientists at the Center for Research and Interdisciplinarity (CRI) in Paris, with collaborating researchers from the USA, Australia, Canada, Costa Rica, India and more! We are interested in bringing in minds from all over the world, yours included, to come up with great ideas to research this virus.
